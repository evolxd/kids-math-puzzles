WORLD 01

称重与信息推理

Weighing & Information Logic 17 题
Lv.1
Three identical-looking balls, one heavier. Find it in a single weighing.
3个外表相同的球，其中1个较重，只称1次，找出较重的球。
Lv.1
With only 1 weighing, what's the largest number of balls (one known heavier) you can always identify? Try 4 balls — can you guarantee finding it in a single weighing? Why not?
只称1次，最多能从几个球里保证找出较重的那个？试试4个球——1次称重能保证找到吗？为什么不行？
Lv.2
Eight balls, one heavier. Find it in two weighings.
8个球中有1个较重，只称2次找出。
Lv.2
Nine balls, one heavier. Find it in two weighings.
9个球中有1个较重，只称2次找出。
Lv.2
Guess a number between 1 and 20, told "higher/lower" each time. Max guesses needed?
1~20之间猜一个数，每次告诉你"大了/小了"，最多几次必猜中？
Lv.2
Guess a number 1-100 with the same feedback. What strategy guarantees success within 7 guesses?
1~100之间猜数字，用什么策略最多7次内必中？
Lv.2
Two boxes, both mislabeled ("red balls"/"blue balls" but swapped). Draw once to fix both labels.
两个盒子标签全部贴错（一个写"红球"一个写"蓝球"，但实际都装反了），只摸1次就能纠正标签，怎么摸？
Lv.3
Three boxes labeled "apples/oranges/mixed" — all wrong. Open one, look at one fruit, deduce all contents.
三个盒子标签全错（"苹果""橙子""混合"），只开1个盒子看1个水果，推出全部盒子内容。
Lv.3
12 coins, one fake and known heavier. Find it in 3 weighings.
12枚硬币中有1枚假币，已知假币更重，3次称重找出。
Lv.3
Weights of 1g, 3g, 9g (usable on either pan) — can you weigh every whole gram from 1 to 13?
3个砝码1克、3克、9克，天平两边都能放砝码，能称出1~13克之间所有整数重量吗？
Lv.4
Weights 1,3,9,27g — can you weigh every integer gram 1-40? Try measuring 5g.
4个砝码1、3、9、27克，能称出1~40克所有整数重量吗？试称出5克。
Lv.4
3 balls, one fake of unknown weight direction. Find it — and whether heavy or light — in 2 weighings.
3个球中有1个假球，不知道它偏重还是偏轻，2次称重找出并判断轻重。
Lv.4
2 eggs, 100 floors. Some floor is the breaking point. Minimum drops to find it for certain?
2个鸡蛋，100层楼，鸡蛋从某层扔下会碎（层数未知），最少扔几次能确定安全楼层？
Lv.4
12 coins, one fake of unknown weight direction. Find it and its direction in 3 weighings.
12枚硬币中有1枚假币，不知偏重偏轻，3次称重找出并判断轻重。
Lv.5
13 coins, one fake (direction unknown), plus one guaranteed-genuine reference coin. Find the fake in 3 weighings — why does the reference coin let you handle one more coin?
13枚硬币，其中1枚假币（不知轻重），但你还有1枚"确定是真的"的标准币可用，3次称重找出假币——说说为什么多了1枚标准币能多称1枚硬币？
Lv.5
10 barrels of pills; one barrel's pills are each 1g light. Using a digital scale just once, find the bad barrel.
10桶药丸，其中1桶每粒都轻1克，正常药丸每粒10克，只用1次称重（电子秤）找出哪一桶是假的。
Lv.5
You've seen that 2 weighings max out at 3 balls, and 3 weighings max out at 12 (unknown direction, no reference coin). Following that pattern, what's the largest number of balls solvable in 4 weighings?
你已经知道2次称重最多能测3个球，3次称重最多能测12个（不知轻重、没有标准币）。按这个规律推算，4次称重最多能测多少个球？
WORLD 02

组合与计数思维

Combinatorics & Counting 16 题
Lv.1
At a party of 8, everyone shakes hands with everyone else once. Total handshakes?
8个人的聚会，每两人握手一次，一共握手多少次？
Lv.1
How many diagonals does a hexagon have?
一个六边形，能画出多少条对角线？
Lv.2
In a 4×4 grid, how many squares of all sizes can you count?
一个4×4的方格网中，一共能数出多少个大小不同的正方形？
Lv.2
On a grid made of 3×3 small squares (so 4×4 dots), moving only right or down along the lines, how many paths from the top-left dot to the bottom-right dot?
在一个由3×3个小方格组成的网格中（即4×4个格点），只能沿线向右或向下走，从左上角走到右下角一共有多少种走法？
Lv.2
10 identical candies split among 3 kids (0 allowed). How many distributions?
10颗一样的糖果分给3个小朋友（可以分到0颗），一共有多少种不同分法？
Lv.3
A pizza cut with 1 line gives 2 pieces, 2 lines give 4. Max for 3 cuts? 4 cuts? Find the pattern.
一个圆形披萨切1刀最多2块，切2刀最多4块，切3刀最多几块？切4刀呢？找出规律。
Lv.3
5 people line up for a photo. How many different orders are possible?
5个人排成一排拍照，一共有多少种不同的排列方式？
Lv.3
Using digits 1-5 (no repeats), how many different 3-digit numbers can be formed?
从1、2、3、4、5这5个数字中选3个组成三位数（不重复用同一数字），一共能组成多少个不同的三位数？
Lv.3
Rolling a die twice, how many outcome-pairs sum to 7?
一个骰子连续掷2次，两次点数之和等于7的组合有多少种？
Lv.4
At the same 8-person party, if 2 married couples skip shaking hands with their own spouse, how many handshakes actually happen?
同样8人的聚会，如果有2对夫妻互相不握手，实际发生了多少次握手？
Lv.4
A cube has 8 vertices. Connecting any 2 with a segment, how many segments of each distinct length exist?
一个正方体的8个顶点，任选2个顶点连一条线段，一共能画出多少条不同长度的线段？
Lv.4
In a 5×5 grid, how many total squares of all sizes?
5×5的方格网中，一共能数出多少个正方形？
Lv.4
Choosing 4 different digits 0-9 to form a 4-digit number (no leading zero), how many are possible?
从0-9中选4个不同数字组成四位数（首位不为0），一共能组成多少个四位数？
Lv.4
From a class of 30, how many ways to choose an unordered group of 3?
一个班级30人，要选出3人组成一个小组（不分职位），一共有多少种选法？
Lv.5
A convex 10-sided polygon — how many diagonals, and the max number of interior intersection points?
一个凸十边形，一共能画出多少条对角线？它们最多能有多少个交点？
Lv.5
5 people sit around a circular table (rotations counted as the same arrangement). How many distinct seatings?
5个人围成一圈坐下（旋转后算同一种排法），一共有多少种不同的坐法？
WORLD 03

逻辑推演谜题

Deductive Logic Puzzles 17 题
Lv.1
Truth-tellers always tell the truth, liars always lie. Someone says "I am a liar." Is this possible? Why?
岛上"诚实人"永远说真话，"说谎者"永远说假话。有人说"我是说谎者"，这句话可能吗？为什么？
Lv.2
Two doors, two guards (one truth-teller, one liar). Ask one question to find the safe door.
两扇门，一扇通向自由一扇通向危险，各站一个守卫（一个只说真话一个只说假话），你只能问1个问题确定安全门，怎么问？
Lv.2
Three in a line, 3 red/2 white hats randomly assigned. The back can't guess; the middle, hearing that, still can't; the front instantly knows. What color?
三个人站成一列。老师有3顶红帽2顶白帽随机给每人戴1顶。最后面的人猜不出，中间的人听到后也猜不出，最前面的人却立刻猜对了。他戴的是什么颜色？
Lv.2
Five people of different heights, given comparative clues. Order them shortest to tallest.
五个人身高各不相同：甲比乙高但比丙矮；丁最矮；戊比甲高但不是最高。请把五人从矮到高排序。
Lv.3
A man on the 10th floor always rides down, but only up to the 7th floor and walks the rest — except on rainy days. Why?
一个人住10楼，每天早上电梯下到1楼上班；下班回来只坐电梯到7楼再走楼梯上去，除非下雨天才直接坐到10楼。为什么？
Lv.3
Three houses in a row: ① the red house is leftmost; ② the dog owner's house is immediately right of the green house; ③ the green house owner doesn't keep a cat. Work out each house's color and pet (red/green/blue; dog/cat/bird).
三栋相邻房子排成一排：①红房子在最左边；②养狗的人家紧挨着绿房子的右边；③绿房子的主人不养猫。请推出每栋房子的颜色和养的宠物（红/绿/蓝三色，狗/猫/鸟三种宠物）。
Lv.3
Three racers A, B, C finish in distinct places. A says "B is not first"; B says "I am first"; C says "A is first." Exactly one of the three is lying. Who is first?
甲乙丙三人赛跑，名次各不相同。甲说："乙不是第一名"；乙说："我是第一名"；丙说："甲是第一名"。已知三人中只有一人说了假话，请问谁是第一名？
Lv.3
Three boxes of two balls each, mislabeled "red-red/blue-blue/one of each." Draw just one ball to deduce all three contents.
三个盒子里各装两个球，标签"红红""蓝蓝""一红一蓝"全部贴错，只摸出1个球，推出三个盒子实际装的是什么。
Lv.3
(Bonus riddle) "The more you wash it, the dirtier it gets — what is it?" Not math, but the same kind of lateral reasoning.
（补充趣味题）一句古老的谜语："有一样东西，越洗越脏，是什么？"——虽然不是数学，但也是一种推理，猜猜看。
Lv.4
Cheryl's birthday is one of: May 15, May 16, May 19, June 17, June 18, July 14, July 16, August 14, August 15, or August 17. She tells the month to Albert and the day to Bernard. Albert says: "I don't know Cheryl's birthday, but I know Bernard doesn't know either." Bernard says: "At first I didn't know, but now I do." Albert says: "Then I know too." What is Cheryl's birthday?
谢丽尔的生日是以下10个日期之一：5月15日、5月16日、5月19日、6月17日、6月18日、7月14日、7月16日、8月14日、8月15日、8月17日。她把月份告诉小明，把日期告诉小华。小明说："我不知道谢丽尔的生日，但我知道小华也不知道。"小华说："一开始我不知道，但现在我知道了。"小明说："那我也知道了。"谢丽尔的生日是哪天？
Lv.4
Four suspects each make one statement; only one is telling the truth. Deduce the culprit.
一个案发现场只有ABCD四个嫌疑人，每人说了一句话，只有一人说真话，推出谁是凶手。
Lv.4
Four houses in a row, each with a different nationality and pet. ① The American lives in house 1; ② the French person lives in house 4; ③ the Chinese person's house is immediately right of the Japanese person's; ④ the American keeps a dog; ⑤ the Chinese person keeps a fish; ⑥ the Japanese person doesn't keep a cat. Who keeps the fish, and who keeps the cat?
四栋房子排成一排，主人国籍和宠物各不相同。①1号房子住着美国人；②4号房子住着法国人；③中国人的房子紧挨着日本人房子的右边；④美国人养狗；⑤中国人养鱼；⑥日本人不养猫。请问谁养鱼？谁养猫？
Lv.4
Five ages multiply to 180, none over 10, positive integers (repeats allowed). Find all combinations and explain why the product alone doesn't pin down a unique answer.
五个人的年龄乘积是180，且没有人超过10岁，年龄都是正整数（可重复）。求所有可能组合，并说明为什么仅凭乘积不能确定唯一答案。
Lv.4
100 lights, all off. Person k toggles every light whose number is a multiple of k. After all 100 people act, how many lights are on?
100盏灯编号1-100全部关闭。第k个人把编号是k的倍数的灯都按一下开关。第100个人操作完后，一共有多少盏灯是亮着的？
Lv.5
The full Einstein's Riddle. 5 houses in a row, each a different color, owner a different nationality, drink, cigarette brand, and pet. ① The Brit lives in the red house. ② The Swede keeps dogs. ③ The Dane drinks tea. ④ The green house is immediately left of the white house. ⑤ The green house owner drinks coffee. ⑥ The Pall Mall smoker keeps birds. ⑦ The yellow house owner smokes Dunhill. ⑧ The person in the middle house drinks milk. ⑨ The Norwegian lives in house 1. ⑩ The Blend smoker lives next to the cat owner. ⑪ The horse owner lives next to the Dunhill smoker. ⑫ The Blue Master smoker drinks beer. ⑬ The German smokes Prince. ⑭ The Norwegian lives next to the blue house. ⑮ The Blend smoker has a neighbor who drinks water. Who owns the fish?
完整版爱因斯坦谜题。5栋房子排成一排，颜色、主人国籍、饮品、香烟、宠物各不相同。①英国人住红房子；②瑞典人养狗；③丹麦人喝茶；④绿房子紧挨在白房子左边；⑤绿房子主人喝咖啡；⑥抽Pall Mall烟的人养鸟；⑦黄房子主人抽Dunhill；⑧住中间房子的人喝牛奶；⑨挪威人住1号房子；⑩抽Blend烟的人和养猫的人是邻居；⑪养马的人和抽Dunhill烟的人是邻居；⑫抽Blue Master烟的人喝啤酒；⑬德国人抽Prince烟；⑭挪威人和蓝房子主人是邻居；⑮抽Blend烟的人和喝水的人是邻居。请问谁养鱼？
Lv.5
100 prisoners in a line wearing black/white hats (see others', not own). Starting from the back, each guesses aloud. Max number guaranteed to guess correctly?
100个囚犯每人头顶1顶帽子（黑或白），只能看到别人的不能看到自己的。从队尾开始依次喊出自己猜测的颜色，其他人都能听到。最多能保证多少人猜对？
Lv.5
Three gods — True, False, Random — identities unknown, answer only "da"/"ja" (meaning unknown). In 3 questions, identify the True god. (The famous "Hardest Logic Puzzle Ever" — bonus challenge.)
三个神：真神永远说真话，假神永远说假话，随机神有时真有时假，你不知道谁是谁，他们只用"da"和"ja"回答（不知哪个代表"是"）。最多问3个问题，找出谁是真神。（这是逻辑学著名难题，作为选做挑战）
WORLD 04

空间与几何思维

Spatial & Geometric Thinking 17 题
Lv.1
Draw 9 dots in a 3×3 grid. Connect all 9 dots using only 4 straight lines, without lifting your pen — the lines can extend beyond the dots.
在纸上画3×3排列的9个点。只用4条连续的直线（中途不能抬笔）把9个点全部连起来——直线可以画到点阵范围外面。
Lv.1
A rectangle twice as long as wide — fold it once (no cutting) into a square shape.
一张长方形纸，长是宽的2倍，只折1次（不剪）折出正方形形状。
Lv.2
At 3:15, what's the angle between the hour and minute hands?
现在是3点15分，时针和分针之间的夹角是多少度？
Lv.2
In 12 hours, how many times do the hour and minute hands overlap?
12小时内，时针和分针一共重合多少次？
Lv.2
The matchstick equation "6+4=4" is wrong. Move one matchstick to make it correct.
用火柴摆出的等式"6+4=4"是错的，只移动1根火柴，让它变成正确的等式。
Lv.2
How many distinct ways can a cube's surface be unfolded flat (net patterns)?
一个正方体的表面展开图有多少种不同的展开方式？
Lv.3
An 8×8 board with two opposite corners removed — can 31 dominoes tile the remaining 62 squares? Why or why not?
一个8×8的棋盘剪掉相对的两个角，能否用1×2骨牌铺满剩下的62格？为什么？
Lv.3
A knight on a chessboard, moving corner to opposite corner — minimum moves?
国际象棋棋盘上，"马"从棋盘一角走到对角的另一角，最少需要走几步？
Lv.3
A 30×12×12cm room; a spider and fly sit at the centers of opposite walls. Crawling along surfaces only, what's the shortest path?
一个长方体房间长30厘米宽12厘米高12厘米，蜘蛛在一面墙正中间，苍蝇在对面墙正中间，蜘蛛只能沿墙爬，最短要爬多远？
Lv.3
A square of paper, cut with one straight line — max pieces? What if you fold it first, then make one cut?
一张正方形纸，只剪1刀（剪直线），最多能把它分成几块？如果允许对折后再剪呢？
Lv.4
With 4 straight lines, what's the max number of regions a plane can be divided into? Generalize to n lines.
用4条直线，最多能把一个平面分成多少个区域？用n条直线呢？找规律。
Lv.4
Slicing a cube with a single flat plane, what's the maximum number of sides the cross-section can have?
一个正方体，用一个平面切一刀，切面最多能是几边形？
Lv.4
A 3×3×3 cube is painted on all faces, then cut into 27 unit cubes. How many have 3, 2, 1, or 0 painted faces?
一个3×3×3的大正方体表面全涂色后切成27个小正方体，三面、两面、一面、没涂色的各有多少个？
Lv.4
Using only the hour hand's angle, can you uniquely determine the exact time to the minute? Why or why not?
一个圆形时钟，只用时针的角度（不看分针），能不能唯一确定当前是几点几分？为什么？
Lv.4
After folding a paper in half n times, how many layers are there in theory? Why can't you actually reach 10 folds?
一张纸对折n次后，理论上有多少层？为什么现实中折不到10次？
Lv.5
On a donut-shaped (toroidal) surface, what's the minimum number of colors needed so no two adjacent regions share a color?
一个甜甜圈形状（环面）表面最少需要几种颜色，才能保证任意两个相邻区域颜色不同？
Lv.5
An ant at one vertex of a unit cube must crawl along the surface only to the opposite vertex. Shortest path length?
一只蚂蚁在正方体的一个顶点，要爬到相对的顶点，只能沿表面爬，棱长为1，最短路径是多少？
WORLD 05

数字游戏与数论

Number Games & Number Theory 16 题
Lv.1
Using 1,2,3,4 once each with +,-,×,÷ and parentheses, make 24. Find multiple solutions.
用1、2、3、4（每个只能用一次），配合+-×÷和括号，让结果等于24，找出几种不同方法。
Lv.2
Pick any number, double it, add 6, halve it, subtract the original — always get 3. Why?
心里想一个数，乘以2，加6，除以2，再减去原来的数，结果永远是3，为什么？
Lv.2
Fill a 3×3 grid with 1-9 so every row, column, and diagonal sums equally (a magic square). How?
一个3×3的方格，把1-9填进去，让每行每列和两条对角线的和都相等（幻方），怎么填？
Lv.2
Find the pattern: 2, 6, 12, 20, 30, __, __ — and give a formula for the nth term.
找规律填空：2, 6, 12, 20, 30, __, __，并说出这个数列的通项公式。
Lv.3
In TWO + TWO = FOUR, each letter is a digit (consistent, distinct, no leading zero). Solve it.
在算式 TWO + TWO = FOUR 中，每个字母代表一个数字，求每个字母代表什么数字。
Lv.3
Under 100, how many numbers are multiples of both 3 and 4?
100以内，有多少个数字既是3的倍数又是4的倍数？
Lv.3
Take any 3-digit number, rearrange its digits, subtract the smaller from the larger — is the result always a multiple of 9? Why?
任意选一个三位数，把它的三个数字重新排列后与原数相减，结果一定是9的倍数吗？为什么？
Lv.3
A palindrome number reads the same forwards and backwards. How many exist between 100 and 1000?
"回文数"从左往右读和从右往左读都一样（比如121、3443）。100到1000之间，一共有多少个回文数？
Lv.4
The magic number 1089: pick a 3-digit number (hundreds digit ≥2 more than units), reverse-subtract, then add the reverse — always 1089. Verify, and think about why.
神奇数字1089：任选一个三位数（百位比个位大2以上），倒过来减去它，再加上倒序，最后总会得到1089。试验证并想想为什么。
Lv.4
A perfect number equals the sum of its proper divisors (6=1+2+3). Verify that 28 is also perfect.
一个数如果等于它所有真因数之和，叫"完全数"，比如6=1+2+3。100以内还有一个完全数是28，试着验证。
